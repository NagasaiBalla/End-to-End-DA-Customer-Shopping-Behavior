# End-to-End-DA-Customer-Shopping-Behavior

## Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

## Project Overview

This end-to-end Data Analytics project analyzes customer shopping behavior using transactional data from 3,900 purchases. The objective is to uncover spending patterns, customer segments, product preferences, subscription behavior, and purchase drivers to support data-driven business decisions.

## Business Problem

How can the company leverage consumer shopping data to identify trends, improve customer engagement, and optimize marketing and product strategies?

## Key Insights

* Female customers generated higher revenue than male customers.
* Express shipping customers spent more per transaction.
* Loyal customers contributed significantly to overall revenue.
* Subscription programs positively influenced customer retention.
* Product ratings and discounts strongly impacted purchase decisions.

## Business Recommendations

* Expand subscription programs.
* Improve customer loyalty initiatives.
* Focus on targeted marketing campaigns.
* Promote top-rated products.
* Optimize discount strategies.
